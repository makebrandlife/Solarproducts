Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from our customers

How long does the installation take? +

A typical residential solar panel system installation takes 2-5 days after the site survey. The time required depends on the roof shape and system size. Systems with batteries require an additional 1-2 days.

Do you help with subsidy applications? +

Yes, we provide complete support for government subsidy applications. We prepare all necessary documents and handle the application process on your behalf. Our success rate is over 99%.

Is maintenance required? +

Solar panels are basically maintenance-free, but cleaning the surface 1-2 times a year helps maintain efficiency. We also offer paid periodic inspection plans.

Can it work during power cuts? +

If you have a battery system, it automatically switches to standalone operation during power cuts, allowing you to use important appliances. Solar-only systems can also generate power when there's sunlight.

What is the payback period? +

Depending on installation conditions and electricity usage, the initial investment is typically recovered in 8-12 years. After that, you can significantly save on electricity bills. Panels have a lifespan of 20+ years.
