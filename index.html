Frequently Asked Questions About Solar Products

How long do solar products typically last?

Quality solar products can last between 5-15 years depending on the type and usage. Solar panels often come with 25-year warranties, while solar lights and chargers typically last 2-5 years with proper maintenance.

Do solar products work on cloudy days?

Yes, though at reduced efficiency. Modern solar panels can still generate 10-25% of their rated capacity on cloudy days. Some products come with battery storage to power devices during periods of limited sunlight.

Are solar products difficult to install?

Most consumer solar products like lights, chargers, and small panels are designed for easy installation without professional help. Larger systems may require expert installation.
